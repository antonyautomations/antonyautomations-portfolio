Smart Lead Deduplication Engine

Businesses often lose trust and waste sales effort when the same lead exists multiple times across CRMs and spreadsheets. This system automatically detects, merges, and cleans duplicate leads in real time—ensuring your sales team always works with clean, reliable data.

Follow-Up Recovery System

Missed follow-ups silently kill revenue. This automation tracks unanswered leads, stalled conversations, and inactive prospects, then triggers timely follow-ups across email or WhatsApp so no opportunity slips through the cracks.

Lead Scoring System

Treating every lead the same slows down growth. This system analyzes lead behavior, engagement, and attributes to automatically prioritize high-intent prospects, helping sales teams focus on deals that are most likely to convert.
